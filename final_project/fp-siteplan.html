Site Name

The Story of My Knives

Site Map

  • Introduction: (As stated below in the Site Purpose and Goals.) I will also introduce myself. I am a middle aged, happily married father of four, working in the healthcare informatics workspace. I grew up carrying and using knives as often as possible. I've casually built a collection of knifes.
  • I like certain knives more than others. Some styles really speak to my personal aesthetic. I hold a deep appreciation for knives as a tool in everyday life, as well as camping and other outdoors activities. I have a very frugal perspective when it comes to knife ownership: I favor models that are easily affordable. I appreciate beauty in the form knifemakers give to their creations. I value knives that are durable, useful, and designed with more than current fashion in mind. While I appreciate inexpensive knives, I also avoid those that clearly are a waste of time and money.
  • I will begin each knife feature with a profile. This will include a picture of the knife, size, configuration, and other details describing the knife.
  • The primary information I want to convey is a story about each knife I own. I will tell the reader how and when I got each knife, what I like about each, and how I like to use this knife. I will include details from the history of my ownership of each example.
  • Generally, I will provide information on caring for a knife, safety rules, and how to sharpen a knife.
  • I may also suggest things the reader may appreciate if they consider owning a knife like mine. This portion of the narrative may or may not include links to current purchase options or to similarly configured knives.

Site Purpose and Goals

I've liked knives since I received my first pocket knife as a Cub
Scout on my 8th birthday. Over the years I have casually collected
many knives. Some were gifts, others purchased or found. Each knife
has a story behind it. I want to tell their stories. I will showcase
my favorite knives and tell you what I like about each.
Would you like to see a few?

This website will provide information and stories behind my favorite knives, such as:

  • "What was the first knife you owned and how old were you when you got it?"
  • "Do you have a favorite knife?"
  • "Do you have different knives for different purposes?"
  • "What are my thoughts on knives as a useful tool?"
  • "Have you ever injured yourself while using a knife?"
  • "Where do you get your knives?"
  • "What are your thoughts on how much to spend on a knife?"

Target Audience
