Site Name

Exciting Expeditions, LLC website

Site Purpose and Goals

This website is meant to advertise the tour guide services, announce expedition packages and pricing, to schedule river running expeditions, to provide information to prepare river-runners, and to handle customer questions. Customers may ask questions by entering into direct communication with staff. This website will provide answers to common questions our customers have, such as:

  • "Why might I want to try a river expedition?"
  • "What rivers may I travel down?"
  • "What river running packages are available?"
  • "How much will this cost?"
  • "What kinds of payment are accepted?"
  • "What will the weather be like during my expedition?"
  • "What gear is provided on my expedition and what else might I need or like to have?"
  • "What should I do to prepare for my expedition?"
  • "What are the risks of river-running?"

Target Audience

The target audience includes but is not limited to vacationers and adventure seekers of all ages and all skill levels. It is meant to provide a broad range of experiences, from comfortable catered trips to rough and rugged expeditions.
