Site Name

Exciting Expeditions, LLC website

Site Purpose and Goals

This website is meant to advertise the tour guide services, to schedule river running experiences, to provide information to prepare river-runners, and to handle customer questions.

Target Audience

The target audience is vacationers and adventure seekers of all ages and all skill levels. It is meant to provide a broad range of experiences, from comfortable catered trips to rough and rugged expeditions.
